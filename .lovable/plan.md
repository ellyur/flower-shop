

# Liceria Rose Flower Shop - Visual Catalog Website

## Overview
A one-page minimalist luxury flower catalog website where customers browse beautiful flower arrangements and order via Facebook Messenger by screenshotting their chosen bouquet.

---

## Design & Aesthetic

**Visual Style:**
- Clean white background with elegant, premium feel
- Serif font (like Playfair Display) for section titles
- Sans-serif font (like Inter) for instructions and body text
- Subtle shadows and smooth transitions
- Calm, romantic, and sophisticated atmosphere inspired by luxury brands

**Color Palette:**
- Pure white background
- Soft rose gold or blush accents
- Dark charcoal text for readability

---

## Page Structure

### 1. Header
- Logo displayed prominently (centered or left-aligned)
- Shop name "Liceria Rose Flower Shop" in elegant serif
- Clean, sticky navigation for easy section jumping

### 2. Hero/Instruction Section
- Welcoming centered text explaining how to order
- "Browse our flower collections below. Screenshot the flower you like and send it to us on Messenger to order."
- Messenger button for quick access

### 3. Flower Catalog Sections

**Grid Layout:**
- 2 columns on mobile devices
- 3-4 columns on desktop
- Clean gaps between images
- Each section starts with an elegant serif title

**Lightbox Feature:**
- Tap any image to open full-screen view
- Easy to screenshot in full quality
- Swipe/arrow navigation between images
- Close button to return to catalog

**Section 1: Carnation Bouquet** (24 bouquets)
- Available colors reference image shown first
- Grid of all carnation arrangements

**Section 2: Local Roses Bouquet** (27 bouquets)
- Available colors reference image shown first
- Grid of all rose arrangements

**Section 3: Special Bouquet - Best Sellers** (22 bouquets)
- Highlighted as popular/featured items
- Grid of all special arrangements

**Section 4: Sunflower Bouquet** (17 bouquets)
- Grid of all sunflower arrangements

**Section 5: Tulips Bouquet** (21 bouquets)
- Available colors reference image shown first
- Grid of all tulip arrangements

### 4. How to Order Section
Simple 4-step visual guide:
1. Browse our website and choose a flower
2. Screenshot the flower image you like
3. Send the screenshot to our Facebook Messenger
4. Wait for order confirmation

### 5. Important Reminders Section
Clean, readable list of policies:
- No specific delivery time guaranteed
- No customized wrapping or arrangements
- No payment, no order
- Orders confirmed after full payment
- Subject to availability; similar replacements may be used
- Delivery fees apply
- Double-check recipient details
- No photo requests during delivery
- No cancellation and no refund once paid

### 6. Footer Call-to-Action
- Bold reminder text: "Screenshot your chosen flower and message us on Facebook Messenger to order."
- Large, prominent Messenger button
- Shop name and logo

---

## User Experience Features

- **Smooth scrolling** between all sections
- **Quick navigation** to jump to any flower category
- **Mobile-optimized** for easy browsing and screenshotting
- **Fast image loading** with optimized Cloudinary images
- **Full-screen lightbox** for detailed viewing and easy screenshots
- **Sticky Messenger button** for quick ordering access

---

## Technical Notes
- No backend needed - purely static catalog
- All prices embedded in images (no text pricing)
- No cart, checkout, or payment processing
- Facebook Messenger integration via simple link
